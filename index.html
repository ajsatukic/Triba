How to Play Triba

Welcome to Triba — a glowing duel of strategy, colors, and triangles! Two players compete by connecting points into triangles. The last player able to move is the winner.

Your Goal

Form valid triangles on the board and outlast your opponent by leaving them with no moves.

Players & Colors

  • Player A → Pink triangles
  • Player B → Cyan triangles

Taking a Turn

  1. Click on three points to select them.
  2. If the triangle is valid:
    • It glows in your color.
    • The points become locked.
    • Turn passes to your opponent.

Valid Triangle Rules

  • Points can’t be in a straight line.
  • You can’t reuse points already locked.
  • Triangle edges cannot cross other triangles.

Invalid Move

If your triangle breaks the rules, a popup will appear. Don’t worry — just try again!

Game Over

The game ends when a player has no possible moves left. That player loses, and the other is crowned champion!

Pro Tips

  • Think ahead: block your opponent’s future options.
  • Keep enough space for your own next move.
  • Every triangle matters — plan wisely!
